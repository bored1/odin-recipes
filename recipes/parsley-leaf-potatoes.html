Parsley Leaf Potatoes

Delightful and cute Parsley Leaf Potatoes

But somewhere between five-ingredient breadcrumbs and recipes that don’t need to be spelled out, there’s this, a potato recipe with only salt, butter and parsley.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces unsalted butter
  • 8 small-medium baking potatoes, scrubbed
  • 16 fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves
  • Coarse salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Place roasting pan in oven with butter; once butter has melted, just a minute or two later, remove pan from oven.
  3. alve each potato lengthwise. Place one parsley leaf in the center of each cut half, then sprinkle cut sides generously with salt.
  4. Arrange face-down in pan with melted butter; try not to nudge them around or the leaves will move (as mine did) off-center. Roast potatoes for 35 to 45 minutes (depending on size) until tender.
